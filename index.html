Before Your Visit

Booking Guidelines

Our policies respect your time, our technicians, and service quality.

Appointments

Appointments are recommended and can be booked online or by phone. Walk-ins are welcome when availability allows.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

We kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling.

Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours' notice and missed appointments (no-shows) may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late, your service time may be adjusted to accommodate the next scheduled appointment. In some cases, services may need to be shortened or rescheduled.

Deposits

Certain services may require a deposit to secure your appointment. Deposits are applied toward your service total and are non-refundable in the event of late cancellation or no-show.

Service Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is important to us. If you have any concerns during your visit, please let us know at the time of service so we can address them promptly.

Health & Safety

We reserve the right to refuse service if health or safety concerns arise. We follow strict sanitation and hygiene standards at all times.

By booking an appointment with 18K Nail Boutique, you acknowledge and agree to this policy.
