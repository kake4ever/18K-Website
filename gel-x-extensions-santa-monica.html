What Are Gel-X Extensions?

Extensions without the damage

Gel-X is a soft gel extension system developed by Aprés Nail. Pre-shaped soft gel tips are bonded to your natural nail using a gel adhesive and cured under LED light — no drilling, no filing into the natural nail, and no harsh acrylic monomer.

The result is a flexible, natural-feeling extension that moves with your nail rather than against it. Gel-X extensions are significantly lighter than acrylic and cause far less stress on the natural nail during both application and removal.

At 18K Nail Boutique in Santa Monica, our technicians are trained in proper Gel-X application technique. We size each tip individually for a perfect fit, ensuring your extensions look seamless and last as long as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Gel-X extensions last?
Gel-X extensions typically last 3 to 5 weeks depending on nail growth and daily activities. They can be filled or removed and reapplied as needed.
Are Gel-X extensions better than acrylic?
For most clients, Gel-X is gentler on natural nails than acrylic. They're lighter, more flexible, and easier to remove without causing significant damage.
Can I get nail art with Gel-X extensions?
Absolutely. Gel-X creates a perfect smooth surface for gel color, ombre, chrome powder, and custom nail art designs.
Is a deposit required?
Yes. A $35 deposit is required for Gel-X full set appointments. The deposit is applied to your total balance and is non-refundable for cancellations within 24 hours.
